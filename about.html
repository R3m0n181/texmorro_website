Production Transparency

Clear Manufacturing Workflow

Our process is structured to ensure each stage is completed in the correct order, maintaining consistency across every production run.

Step : 01

Product Concept or Tech Pack Review

We review your product idea or tech pack and align on specifications, materials, and construction.

Step : 02

Fabric & Material Planning

Fabrics are sourced and verified against the product requirements before sampling begins.

Step : 03

Sample Development

A production sample is prepared, reviewing fit, construction, and branding details.

Step : 04

Sample Approval

The sample is reviewed and approved — this becomes the quality reference for all bulk production.

Step : 05

Bulk Production

Manufacturing proceeds under monitored stages with systematic quality checks throughout.

Step : 06

Final Quality Review

A final check confirms construction, branding, and consistency before dispatch.
